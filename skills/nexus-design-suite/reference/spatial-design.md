# Spatial Design

## Goals
- Make structure obvious through rhythm, grouping, and scale.
- Keep layouts stable across breakpoints.

## Guidelines
- Use a spacing scale and stick to it.
- Build layout from edges, columns, and content relationships rather than arbitrary card stacks.
- Let white space create importance and calm.
- Use alignment to show what belongs together and what does not.

## Avoid
- Cards inside cards inside panels.
- One-off spacing values that fight the system.
- Centering everything when the content wants structure.
