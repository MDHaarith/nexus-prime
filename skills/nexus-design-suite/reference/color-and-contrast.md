# Color And Contrast

## Goals
- Use color to drive hierarchy, affordance, and product personality.
- Preserve WCAG AA contrast as the baseline.

## Guidelines
- Build palettes from tinted neutrals plus a deliberate accent family.
- Use semantic colors for success, warning, and failure states that stay legible in every theme.
- Check contrast against the actual background, not a palette swatch.
- Prefer tonal systems and variables over hard-coded hex values.

## Avoid
- Pure black, pure gray, or muddy low-chroma text on tinted surfaces.
- Gradient-heavy hero treatments as a substitute for hierarchy.
- Accent color everywhere. It should still mean something.
